Rivian Automotive, Inc. is an American electric vehicle manufacturer and automotive technology company.
Founded in 2009 by Robert "RJ" Scaringe, it is headquartered in Irvine, California.
Rivian's main products are the R1T electric pickup truck and the R1S electric SUV. These vehicles are based on a "skateboard" platform and emphasize off-road performance and long-range driving capabilities.
Rivian has attracted investments from major companies such as Amazon and Ford, and has also signed a contract to supply electric delivery vans to Amazon.
It was listed on the NASDAQ in late 2021 and received significant attention.
