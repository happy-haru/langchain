Tesla, Inc. is an American electric vehicle and clean energy company.
It was co-founded in 2003 by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning. Elon Musk was an early investor and currently serves as the company's CEO and product designer. Its headquarters are in Austin, Texas.
Tesla's representative electric vehicle models include the Model S (sedan), Model 3 (sedan), Model X (SUV), Model Y (compact SUV), and Cybertruck (pickup truck).
Tesla also produces energy-related products such as solar panels, the Powerwall, a home energy storage system, and the Megapack, a large-scale energy storage system.
It is leading the innovation of the electric vehicle market and is also investing heavily in the development of autonomous driving technology.
